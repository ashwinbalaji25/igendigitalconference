About IGEN Nobel Laureate Lecture Online Competition

THE INSTITUTION OF GREEN ENGINEERS

IGEN is launching its first Nobel Laureate Lecture Series on 25th Aug 2020 by Nobel Laureate Sir Richard John Roberts - 1993 Nobel Laureate in Medicine or Physiology.

In context with the event, IGEN Nobel Laureate Lecture Series (NLLS) Team announces An Awe-Inspiring International Multifold Competitions for people of all ages. The details on Who, What, Where& How of the Competitions are mentioned below in this website.

Please Check in, Publish your Ideas on Category based on your Eligibility and Win a medal with an E-Certificate holding the Signature of Nobel Laureate Sir Richard J. Roberts. Winning entries will be showcased during the live streaming of the event and also be published on “IGEN Nobel Laureate Lecture Series” website.
